🌱 Week 1 — Animal Friends
Yogi Joy Yogi Hope

🧗 Welcome, Little Yogis!

Meet Yogi Joy and her twin sister Yogi Hope! Joy loves to jump and play, and Hope loves to breathe and stay calm. Together, they're taking you on a yoga adventure with animal friends! Click each animal below to learn the pose!

Cat & Cow

Kitty Cat Stretch
▼

Joy says:

  1. Get down on your hands and knees, like a kitty cat! Hands under your shoulders, knees under your hips.
  2. Scared kitty! Push your back up reeeally high and look down at your tummy! Breathe Out
  3. Happy cow! Let your tummy go down and look up at the ceiling! Breathe In
  4. Keep going! Scared kitty... happy cow! You can meow and moo! Do it 5 times!

Hope says:

  1. Now let's add the breathing. Scared kitty — breathe out real slow. Haaaah.
  2. Happy cow — breathe in real big. Mmmmm.
  3. Two more together. Scared kitty... out... happy cow... in. Beautiful!
Listen
🍎 Teacher Tip: Invite children to make soft "meow" sounds on the cat stretch and "moo" on the cow. This helps cue the breathing rhythm naturally.
🌳

Tree Pose

Tall Tall Tree
▼

Joy says:

  1. Stand up real tall! Like, the tallest tree you ever saw!
  2. Pick up one foot and put it on your other ankle. Like a kickstand on a bike!
  3. If you're a little wobbly, that's okay! Even real trees sway in the wind!
  4. Arms up super high — those are your tree branches! Wiggle your fingers — those are your leaves!

Hope says:

  1. Find something to look at on the wall. Just one spot. That helps you balance.
  2. Let's breathe. One... two... three... four... five.
  3. Now switch feet! Other side. Arms up, wiggle those leaves!
  4. One... two... three... four... five. You are the most beautiful tree!
Listen
🍎 Teacher Tip: "Kickstand" (toes still on the floor) is a great modification. Encourage them to find a "focus spot" on the wall.
🐸

Frog Pose

Hoppy Frog
▼

Joy says:

  1. Stand with your feet kinda wide and turn your toes out. Like frog feet!
  2. Squaaaat down real low. Hands together by your heart.
  3. Now we HOP! Jump! RIBBIT! Land super soft, like you're on a lily pad!
  4. Five hops! Jump, ribbit! Jump, ribbit! Jump, ribbit! Jump, ribbit! One more BIG one!

Hope says:

  1. Squat back down, little froggies. That was a lot of hopping!
  2. Rest like a frog on a lily pad. Close your eyes.
  3. Three belly breaths. In... big tummy... out. In... out. One more... in... out. Breathe
Listen
🍎 Teacher Tip: Great for releasing energy mid-session. Remind children to land softly like they're on a lily pad!
🐍

Cobra Pose

Sneaky Snake
▼

Joy says:

  1. Lie on your tummy. Legs straight behind you. Hands flat next to your shoulders.
  2. Breathe in and push up! Lift your head and chest — just a little! Breathe In
  3. Shoulders down, away from your ears. Look up and go HISSSSSS! Breathe Out
  4. Slowly down. Do it 3 times! Push up... HISSSSSS... and down!

Hope says:

  1. Stay on your tummy. Rest your cheek on your hands.
  2. Feel how warm your back feels? Your muscles worked hard!
  3. Two easy breaths. In... and out. In... and out. Breathe
Listen
🍎 Teacher Tip: Press hip bones into the mat, keep shoulders relaxed. A small lift is perfect — no full extension at this age.
🦋

Butterfly Pose

Flutter Flutter
▼

Joy says:

  1. Sit down and put your feet together. Like your feet are giving each other a high five!
  2. Hold your toes. Let your knees go out — those are your butterfly wings!
  3. Flap them! Up and down! Go fast! Flutter flutter flutter!
  4. Now super slow. Uuuup... and dooown. Uuuup... and dooown.

Hope says:

  1. Breathe in... breathe out and lean forward a teensy bit. Smell a flower! Breathe Out
  2. What color are your wings? Joy's are teal! Mine are coral!
  3. Keep flapping slow. In... out. In... out. In... out. Breathe
Listen
🍎 Teacher Tip: Ask children to name the color and pattern of their butterfly wings to engage imagination and settle energy.

🌙 Cool-Down: Sleepy Star

Joy Hope

Joy: We did SO good today! High five! Lie down on your back and stretch out like a big star! A sleepy star. Hope is gonna help you relax now.

Hope: Close your eyes. Shhh. Feel your tummy going up... and down. You are so strong. You are so brave. You are so awesome. Take five slow breaths... wiggle your fingers and toes... and give yourself the biggest hug ever. You are a YOGA STAR!

Joy: Namaste, friends!
Hope: That means the light in me...
Joy: ...sees the light in YOU!
Hope: See you next time! 🙏
